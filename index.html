Hajans - FWD X Parley

Designed for champions, worn by winners

The FWD X Parley is a high-performance sport shoe that combines cutting-edge design with sustainable materials. Made in collaboration with Parley for the Oceans, the shoe features an upper crafted from recycled ocean plastic, helping to reduce waste and protect our planet.

The FWD X Parley is engineered to provide exceptional support and comfort for all your athletic pursuits. The shoe's dynamic design includes a breathable mesh upper, lightweight cushioning, and a responsive sole for optimal traction and durability.

With its bold style and innovative construction, the FWD X Parley is the perfect shoe for athletes who demand the best in both performance and sustainability. Whether you're hitting the track or the trails, the FWD X Parley has got you covered. So step up your game and join the movement towards a more sustainable future with the FWD X Parley sport shoe.

Designed by John Parley
